## What is this package?

Interactive tools and datasets to explore meteorological context relevant to Australian bushfires.

- Shiny app launcher: `run_ausbushfire_app()`
- Datasets: `climate_data`, `fwi_data`
- Helpers: `calculate_statistics()`, `annual_trends()`, `extreme_events()`

## Get started

```r
install.packages("remotes")
remotes::install_github("ETC5523-2025/assignment-4-packages-and-shiny-apps-rabbit292929/ausbushfire")
library(ausbushfire)
run_ausbushfire_app()
```
